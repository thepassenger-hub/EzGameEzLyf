Who are you?

EzGameEzLyf is a website that compares video game prices from the most popular e-vendors.

When you want to buy a video game nowadays you spend a lot of time surfing store websites to compare prices. We save you the time by automating these tasks.

Which stores do you take your data from?

At the moment these are the stores supported:

  • Steam Store
  • HumbleBundle Store
  • DlGamer
  • GamersGate
  • GreenManGaming
  • GOG
  • GamesPlanetUK
  • GamesPlanetDE
  • GamesPlanetFR
  • Indiegala
  • WinGameStore
